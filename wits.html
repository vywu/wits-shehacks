building a future
for women* in tech

Our purpose is to build a pipeline for women to enter and succeed within the tech industry. At WITS, we aim to increase gender diversity and inclusion in the tech industry by creating a supportive and empowering community for women* interested in tech.

*Any female-identifying, non-binary individual. WITS welcomes all genders and backgrounds to events with the exception of SheHacks, our only non cis-men only event.

WITS's three pillars

Here's what we believe in:

empowering women

We empower forward-thinking women to rapidly learn, test new skills, and gain valuable experiences through our events.

building networks

Our inclusive community allows women to share wisdom and key industry insights with each other.

creating pipelines

We equip women with resources, opportunities, and mentors to achieve success, levelling the playing field for all future women+ to come.
